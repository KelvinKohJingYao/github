About Us

MADISON is a brand name that was conceived and created on 1st March 2013 to expand and consolidate 12 over years of experience in the beauty industry to provide latest and various ranges of beauty products and services. 

Our main objective is to provide a one stop beauty solution to our customers in and surrounding Klang Valley areas including Puchong, Cyberjaya and Putrajaya. Apart from offering superior and comprehensive facial therapy, we also offer other beauty services amongst others like eyelash extensions, waxing and semi-permanent embroidery services for eyebrow, eyeliner and lips. 

MADISON has a warm and friendly environment to have all your beauty needs taken care and pampered of by our experience and talented professional beauticians which is dedicated in providing our customers with the top notch services that we are known.

We believe that your skin is an important asset that will give you the appearance and self-confidence in facing your daily life. Therefore we are using cutting edge technology and specially formulated skin care products to cater the Asian’s skin to suit our climate, lifestyle and harsh environment.
